About us

At RainyDays, we are passionate about the outdoors. Located in Trondheim, we have been offering the highest quality jackets for skiing, canoeing, camping, and hiking for over 10 years. We know that the right gear is essential for enjoying the outdoors and that is why we are dedicated to providing the best quality jackets for all your outdoor adventures. Whether you are looking for a lightweight jacket for a weekend camping trip or an insulated jacket for your next ski vacation, we have you covered. With our jackets, you can explore the great outdoors with confidence and comfort. So don't let the weather stop you from enjoying the beauty of nature. Step outside and start pushing the comfort zone with RainyDays!
